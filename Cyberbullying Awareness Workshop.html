Introduction To Cyberbullying

What Is Cyberbullying

Cyber Bullying is an act of bullying or harassment using electronic means and social media platforms. This is common among teenagers, bullies, or harassers on the internet and other online platforms, particularly social media sites. Bullying behavior can include posting rumors, threatening messages, sexual remarks, a personal information of the victim, or a pejorative label. Bullying or harassment can be identify as a repeated behavior or intent to harm.

Top 5 Countries with the highest rate of Cyber Bullying as of 2018:

  • Unites States of America
  • Brazil
  • South Africa
  • Belgium

Difference Of Joke And CyberBullying

Joke

A joke is given between two people with close relationships with one another. A joke must not be insulting or degrading to a person , if not it shall be considered bullying.

CyberBullying

Bullying is being insulting or annoying to an individual, it lowers a person's self esteem and confidence.When not taken action immediately, a person can suffer from depression or anxiety.

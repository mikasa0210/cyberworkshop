About

What are the goals?

  • To teach people the basics of programming
  • To give young adults an incredibly important life skill that can net them well paying jobs
  • To enlighten young adults on the topic of cyberbullying and to teach them the different methods they can use to prevent and protect themselves from cyberbullying

Where and When is this happening?

The workshop will be held via Zoom meeting every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday of the week from 3:00pm-5:00pm
